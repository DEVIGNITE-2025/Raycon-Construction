Our Story

Quietly building Johannesburg's finest homes since 1982.

Raycon was established in 1982, specialising in alterations and additions in the domestic housing market. In 1988, we moved into the construction of quality homes and alterations in the northern suburbs of Johannesburg.

The majority of our contracts have been built in suburbs such as Dainfern, Dainfern Valley, Fourways Gardens, Broadacres, Waterfall Estate and Bryanston. We pride ourselves on delivering excellent personalised customer service, good value for money, and the construction of new homes within the prescribed time constraint.

Our project capacity is between 5 and 7 projects at a time — depending on size and complexity — ensuring every client receives the attention and dedication their build deserves.

The Journey

Milestones that shaped Raycon

From a small alterations specialist to one of the northern suburbs' most trusted residential builders.

  1. 1982

    Company Founded

    Raycon is established, specialising in alterations and additions in the domestic housing market.

  2. 1988

    Quality Homes in the Northern Suburbs

    Raycon Developments moves into the construction of quality homes in Johannesburg's northern suburbs — establishing a presence in Bryanston, Fourways and Lonehill.

  3. 2009

    Secure Estate Developments

    The start of an era of secure estate work. Since 2009 we've completed:

    • Thorn Bush Estate — Lonehill · 22 homes Down To Earth Architects & Interiors
    • Bramble Bush Estate — Broadacres · 40 homes Down To Earth Architects & Interiors
    • Cowley Row · 6 homes Venui Architects
    • Porchester Place · 5 homes Plan Studio
    • Mount Street · 4 homes Venui Architects
    • The Wahlberg · 11 homes Darryl Croome Architects
  4. Today

    Continued Growth

    We continue to deliver premium residential projects across Dainfern, Waterfall Estate, Bryanston, Craighall, Blue Hills and the wider northern suburbs — spanning new builds, alterations, estate developments and complete turnkey projects.
