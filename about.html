About Raycon

Four Decades of Building Excellence

Since 1982, Raycon Construction has built a reputation for quality, reliability, and craftsmanship in Johannesburg's most sought-after residential suburbs.

Our Story

A timeline of milestones that have shaped Raycon Construction into the company it is today.

Raycon Construction was founded in 1982 with a clear vision: to build homes of exceptional quality in Johannesburg. What began as a small operation has grown into one of the most trusted residential construction companies in the northern suburbs.

Our journey has been defined by a commitment to craftsmanship, transparent communication, and an unwavering focus on delivering homes that stand the test of time. From our early projects through to today's complex estate developments, the same principles guide every decision we make.

1982
Company Founded
Raycon Construction is established, beginning its journey in the residential construction industry in Johannesburg.
1988
Northern Suburbs Focus
Raycon moves into the construction of quality homes in the northern suburbs of Johannesburg, establishing itself in areas like Bryanston, Fourways, and Lonehill.
2007
First Secure Estate Development
Completion of our first secure residential development in Beverley — Thornbush Estates — a community of 22 quality homes. This milestone marked our entry into estate-scale development.
Present
Continued Growth
Today we continue to deliver premium residential projects across Dainfern, Dainfern Valley, Fourways Gardens, Broadacres, Waterfall Estate, Bryanston, and the wider northern suburbs. Our services span new builds, renovations, estate developments, and complete turnkey projects.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every project we undertake.

Quality Without Compromise

Every material is selected with care, every joint checked, every finish inspected. We don't cut corners because we understand that a home is the most significant investment most people will ever make. Our quality standards are non-negotiable — from foundation to roof ridge.

Timelines You Can Trust

We understand the impact that delays have on budgets and lives. Our detailed project planning, experienced site management, and proactive problem-solving mean we consistently deliver projects on or ahead of schedule. When we commit to a date, we mean it.

Clear Communication

Building a home involves hundreds of decisions. We keep you informed at every stage with regular progress updates, clear documentation, and a dedicated point of contact. No surprises, no confusion — just transparent collaboration from start to finish.

Expert Craftsmanship

Our teams bring decades of hands-on experience to every project. From brickwork and plastering to cabinetry and tiling, skilled tradespeople execute every detail with pride. The difference is in the details — and our clients notice.

Our Approach to Safety & Compliance

Safety on our construction sites is paramount. Every Raycon project operates under a comprehensive safety management plan that meets or exceeds industry regulations. Our site teams undergo regular safety training, and we conduct scheduled inspections to maintain the highest standards of workplace safety.

We are fully compliant with all relevant building regulations, NHBRC requirements, and municipal bylaws. Our administrative team manages all necessary permits, approvals, and inspections to ensure that your project proceeds smoothly and legally from day one.

Environmental responsibility is also part of our approach. Where possible, we specify sustainably sourced materials, plan for energy efficiency, and manage construction waste responsibly. We believe that building well means building with care — for both our clients and the broader community.
