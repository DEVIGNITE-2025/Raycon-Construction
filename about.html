About Raycon

Four Decades of Building Excellence

Since 1982, Raycon Developments has built a reputation for quality, reliability, and craftsmanship in Johannesburg's most sought-after residential suburbs.

Our Story

A timeline of milestones that have shaped Raycon Developments into the company it is today.

Raycon was established in 1982, specialising in alterations and additions in the domestic housing market. In 1988, Raycon Developments moved into the construction of quality homes and alterations in the northern suburbs of Johannesburg.

The majority of our contracts have been constructed in up-market suburbs such as Dainfern, Dainfern Valley, Fourways Gardens, Broadacres, Waterfall Estate and Bryanston. We pride ourselves in delivering excellent personalised customer service, good value for money and the construction of new homes and alterations within the prescribed time constraint.

Our project capacity is between 5 and 7 projects depending on the size and complexity of each build, ensuring every client receives the attention and dedication their project deserves.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every project we undertake.

Personalised Customer Service

We pride ourselves on delivering excellent personalised customer service. Each project gets the dedicated attention it deserves, with direct communication through Stephen Rayner as Project Manager overseeing all work.

Good Value for Money

We deliver good value for money on every project. Our long-standing relationships with trusted sub-contractors — built over 30 years — allow us to provide competitive pricing without compromising on quality.

On-Time Delivery

We are committed to the construction of new homes and alterations within the prescribed time constraint. Our detailed project planning and experienced site management mean we consistently deliver on schedule.

Experienced Teams

Most of our employees have worked with us for over 15 years. We have first-rate working relationships with our sub-contractors and have built solid associations with Engineers, Electricians, Plumbers and our Brick Laying, Plastering and Tiling teams over the past 30 years.
